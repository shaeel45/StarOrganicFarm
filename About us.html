Star. Organic. Farm.

Star Organic farm is commenced in the year 1988. Company is involved in trading and manufacturing a wide range of Organic Products and spices to the consumers all around the global market. Company has wide variety of collection of products lies of Nutritious cereals, Pulses, Spices and Condiments, Cooking Oils, Fruit Pulps, Agro Products, Oils, Wheat and Wheat Flour, Rice and Rice products etc.


We work to increase access of local organic produce through farmers markets, pop-ups, delivery, and our local foods co-op.The caring atmosphere we have established for our clients to develop and learn is reflected in our regenerative growth techniques.

A Message from our Founder and Director

At Star Organic Farm, we're trying to make it easy for you to be more sustainable-whether that's supporting local areas or letting us help you grow your own food and raise your own chickens right here in the city. Our goal is to grow more foods, and then next generation of local food advocates and entrepreneurs.

Please enjoy our intentionally curated selection of vegetables, fruit, flowers and variety of collection of products lies of Nutritious cereals, Pulses, Spices and Condiments, Cooking Oils, Fruit Pulps, Agro Products, Oils, Wheat and Wheat Flour, Rice and Rice products etc from our farm.

Our farm family is an inclusive and beautiful mix of children, mothers, youth, adults, caregivers-learning and eating and applying artisanal knowledge of crop care and cultivation, never applying chemicals or pesticides on the soil.

Our regenerative growing practices reflect the nurturing environment we've created for our clients to learn and heal. Thank you for supporting your local, and we look forward to seeing you in the garden soon!
